Rules:
Get a total value as close to 21 as possible without going over.
Your score must be higher than all the other players to win.
Card values:
- A can be 1 or 11 points (automatically adjusted)
- 2-10 are face values
- J, G, K are all 10 points

Each player starts by drawing one card.
On your turn, you can choose to DRAW or STAND (Note that is you choose to STAND when your score is lowest, you lose)

To win, you either get 21 points or be the last player alive(if all other players are over 21)
If you go over 21 points, you lose instantly. If another player got 21 points, you lose too.

HAVE FUN!
