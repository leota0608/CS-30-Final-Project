**Card Descriptions**
Basic Cards:
- Slash: Choose one opponent to attack with Slash. The opponent must play a Slash in response or lose 1 health point.
- Dodge: Used to block a Slash.
- Peach: Restore 1 health point.

Equipment Cards:
- Crossbow: While equipped, the player is not restricted to playing only one Slash per turn.
- Crossblade: While equipped, the player may lose 1 health point to deal 1 damage to an opponent.
- Evasion: While equipped, the player has a 50% chance to automatically dodge an opponent's Slash.

Trick Cards:
- Duel: Choose one opponent to duel. The two players alternately play Slash cards.
  The first player who cannot play a Slash loses 1 health point.
- Dismantle: Choose one opponent and discard one of their hand cards or equipped cards.
- Snatch: Choose one opponent and take one of their hand cards or equipped cards.
- Archery: All other players must play a Dodge; any player who does not loses 1 health point.
- Savage: All other players must play a Slash; any player who does not loses 1 health point.
- Benevolence: Play this card to draw two additional hand cards.
- Negate: Can be played when targeted by a trick card to cancel its effect.
